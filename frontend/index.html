    Step 1: Feature Scaling & Normalization

    Transforms raw spend ($8,500) and recency (8d) into standard z-score vectors.

    Computes churn risk index and checks for discount abuser or fraud behavior.

    Learn how to navigate and use Customer-Seg in 3 simple steps

    Step 2: Test Single Customers & Anomaly Detection
